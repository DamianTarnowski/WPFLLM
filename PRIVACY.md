# Privacy Policy for WPFLLM - AI Assistant

**Last updated: January 2, 2026**

## Overview

WPFLLM ("the App") is a desktop AI assistant application developed by Damian Tarnowski. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

### What We DON'T Collect
- The App does NOT collect any personal information
- The App does NOT send telemetry or analytics data
- The App does NOT track your usage
- The App does NOT share your data with third parties

### What Stays on Your Device
All data created and used by the App is stored **locally on your device**:
- Chat conversations
- Imported documents
- Knowledge base embeddings
- Application settings
- API keys (stored locally, optionally encrypted)

## Third-Party Services

### OpenRouter API
The App uses OpenRouter API to communicate with AI models (GPT-4, Claude, etc.). When you send a message:
- Your message and conversation context are sent to OpenRouter
- OpenRouter processes your request and returns AI responses
- OpenRouter's privacy policy applies: https://openrouter.ai/privacy

You can review OpenRouter's data handling practices on their website.

### Local Embeddings
When using local embedding models, all processing happens on your device. No data is sent to external servers.

## Data Storage

- All data is stored in a local SQLite database
- Optional encryption is available for sensitive data
- You can delete all data by removing the App's data folder

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data:
- Export your conversations at any time
- Delete individual conversations or all data
- Disable/enable encryption as needed

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on our GitHub repository.
